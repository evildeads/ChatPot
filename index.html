ChatPot

What?

ChatPot is a new and innovative chatbot that analyses its conversations with you and trains to talk just like you.

When?

ChatPot is expected to release in late may of 2023 but there may be preorders and testers before then.

Why?

ChatPot is completely customiseable to your needs. ChatPot can answer simple questions and have long conversations with you after very small amounts of training and talking.
